Art Gallery

Wanderlust is not like your average hotel, it is extremely unique. For example, we have an art gallery inside! We buy art from local aspiring artist and hang them in the the gallery to help them get known. The gallery is free with a booking of a hotel room. Wanderlust appreciates art and the creators and we want you to appreciate with us!
